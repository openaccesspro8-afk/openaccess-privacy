## Overview

OpenAccess Pro is a browser extension that provides real-time web accessibility customisation for users with disabilities. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

**The short version: We collect nothing. We store nothing on our servers. All your settings stay on your device.**

---

## Data We Collect

OpenAccess Pro does **not** collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

- Personal identifiable information (name, email, address)
- Browsing history or web activity
- Health information
- Financial or payment information
- Authentication credentials
- Location data
- Website content you visit
- User behaviour or click data

---

## Local Storage

Your accessibility preferences and profile settings are saved **locally on your device only** using the browser's built-in storage API. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

---

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why It's Used |
|---|---|
| `activeTab` | To apply accessibility settings to the page you are currently viewing |
| `scripting` | To inject font, colour, contrast and layout modifications into web pages |
| `sidePanel` | To display the accessibility control panel |
| `storage` | To save your preferences locally on your device |
| `host permissions` | To apply accessibility customisations on any website you visit |

---

## Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties. We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services within the extension.

---

## Children's Privacy

OpenAccess Pro does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.
